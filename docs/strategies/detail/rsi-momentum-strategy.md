# RSIMomentumStrategy

- Code: `BinanceTestnet/Strategies/RSIMomentumStrategy.cs`
- Goal: Momentum continuation using RSI midline bias.

## Indicators
- RSI

## Long entries — where and why
- RSI crosses up through ~50–55 with supportive price structure
- Rationale: align with building bullish momentum.

## Short entries — where and why
- RSI crosses down through ~45–50 with lower-high structure
- Rationale: align with bearish momentum continuation.
