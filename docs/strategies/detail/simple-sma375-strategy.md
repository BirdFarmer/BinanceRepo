# SimpleSMA375Strategy

- Code: `BinanceTestnet/Strategies/SimpleSMA375Strategy.cs`
- Goal: Macro trend regime switching using a very long SMA.

## Indicators
- SMA(375)

## Long entries — where and why
- Close above SMA(375) after extended period below
- Rationale: regime change to bullish bias.

## Short entries — where and why
- Close below SMA(375) after extended period above
- Rationale: regime change to bearish bias.
